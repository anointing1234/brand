AUTHOR & BRAND STRATEGIST

Kevwe Modupe

Kevwe Modupe is a Nigerian Media Entrepreneur, Brand Strategist, Creative Director, Conversation Strategist and A Storyteller. He is the Chairman of TSLNIGERIA, a visionary and a reliable digital and branding company for world-class brands while helping African startups with the online presence required for global visibility. Mr Kevwe Modupe created this encyclopedia to answer a question so many struggle with: “How do I show up and stand out without losing myself?” As the Chairman of TSLNIGERIA, he has vast experience in Creative Directing, Storytelling and developing strategies, and is always ready to bridge the gap in the digital world.
