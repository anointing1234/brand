AUTHOR & BRAND STRATEGIST

Kevwe Modupe

Kevwe Modupe is a Nigerian media entrepreneur, brand strategist, creative director, conversation strategist, and storyteller with a strong passion for digital innovation and human capital development. He is the Chairman of TSLNigeria, a pioneering digital content and branding company specializing in corporate branding, web and mobile app development, digital marketing, advertising, public relations, and integrated marketing solutions. Under his leadership, TSLNigeria has become a trusted partner for world-class brands and African startups, helping them achieve global visibility through strategic digital presence and impactful storytelling. Kevwe Modupe is a Computer Science graduate from the prestigious Federal Polytechnic, Auchi, and a Professional Diploma in Public Relations. He is an Associate Member of the Nigerian Institute of Public Relations (NIPR) and has also been conferred with a Doctor of Technology (DTech, Honoris Causa) by Shama Wordbase University. His career spans over two decades, beginning with his role as General Manager at Unival Computers (2005–2015)before founding TSLNigeria in 2015. Since then, he has built a reputation for his innovative approach to branding, storytelling, and leadership. He is recognized for his ability to organically grow businesses, train leaders, and design strategies that bridge gaps in the evolving digital landscape. Beyond business, Kevwe Modupe is deeply invested in human capital development. He has trained and coached over 100 individuals, addressed audiences of more than 3000 people, and facilitated leadership programs across Nigeria. He also serves as a Facilitator at the Miracle Invasion Leadership Institute (MILI), founded by Pastor Jeffrey Iyonawan. Kevwe Modupe is well-traveled, a devoted husband to Sarah Modupe, and father to two sons, Ferdinard and Leonard. He is highly regarded for his humanitarian contributions, thought leadership, and consistent impact in the digital and branding space.
